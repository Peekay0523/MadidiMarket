Last updated: February 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Madidi Market. By registering your business on our platform, you agree to be bound by these Terms and Conditions. Please read them carefully before registering your business.

2. Platform Fee

As a business owner on Madidi Market, you agree to pay a 5% fee on your profits generated through the platform. This fee is calculated weekly and is due every Friday.

Important: The 5% fee is calculated based on your completed orders (orders with status "completed" or "delivered") for each week.

3. Payment Schedule

Payment Deadline: All platform fees must be paid by 11:59 PM every Friday for the previous week's earnings.

Payment Methods: You can pay the platform fee via:

  • Credit Card (instant processing)
  • Bank Transfer (proof of payment required)

4. Consequences of Non-Payment

Failure to pay the weekly platform fee by Friday will result in your business being removed from the Madidi Market platform.

If your business is removed due to non-payment:

  • Your business profile will be deactivated
  • Your products and services will no longer be visible to customers
  • You will not be able to receive new orders
  • To reinstate your business, all outstanding fees must be paid in full

5. Business Registration Requirements

To register your business on Madidi Market, you must:

  • Provide accurate and complete business information
  • Set your business location on the map
  • Provide valid contact information (email and phone number)
  • Agree to these Terms and Conditions

6. Business Approval

All new business registrations are subject to admin approval. Your business will be reviewed before it becomes visible to customers. You will receive an email notification once your business is approved.

8. Account Security

You are responsible for maintaining the security of your account credentials. Do not share your login information with others.
